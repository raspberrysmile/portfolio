Projects

YourSingapore.com & Business Travel and Meetings, Incentives, Conventions and Exhibitions (BTMICE) portals

Client: Singapore Tourism Board (STB)
Involvement: Frontend Development for AEM Integration
Year: 2014, 2015

Skills: gulp nodeJS Adobe Experience Manager (AEM)
Design System

YourSingapore.com was the portal aimed at promoting tourism in Singapore, while the BTMICE portal was aimed at promoting Singapore as the destination for business travel.

The objective for this project was not only to make the portals user friendly, but also ensured the administrators could easily maintain the website.

We built this site to be as modular as possible, where the clients could drag and drop and use any component they wanted to build a webpage.

To keep this as light as possible, we developed our own frontend framework/design system instead of using the popular bootstrap, jQuery UI, etc.

I worked with the UI designers to ensure the consistency of the UI and also closely with the backend developers to ensure the integration into AEM works as expected.
